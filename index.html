Javier E. Gomez

California Real Estate Agent

Javier Gomez has been in the real estate business for over 20 years. He was born in Colombia, South America and educated in Dearborn, Michigan. His approach in the real estate business is a client- first philosophy combined with an extensive knowledge of the market. He strives to meet each client’s needs with the highest quality of service, while continuing to educate himself on market conditions, purchases, short sales, foreclosures, and REO properties. He also has past experience in working with the banks and courts as a receivership during the real estate crisis.

Selecting a real estate agent is easy, finding one you can trust to understand your specific needs and provide you with a strategy that yields successful results is critical. Javier’s skill set and passion for the job allows him to provide investors and buyers the best possible return on their investments and help first time home buyers to purchase the home of their dreams. He consistently proves to be a strong negotiator creating Successful Real Estate Experiences for his clients in the Northern California region stretching from the bay area to the Sacramento valley.
